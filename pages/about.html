Our Story

Three decades of Italian culinary tradition

Since 1985

A Family Legacy

It all began in a small kitchen in Naples, Italy, where our founder, Giuseppe Romano, discovered his grandmother's treasured recipe book. Filled with handwritten notes and family secrets, these recipes became the foundation of what would eventually become Sapore Ristorante.

In 1990, Giuseppe opened the first Sapore in Rome, bringing his grandmother's recipes to life. The restaurant quickly gained recognition for its authentic flavors and warm hospitality. By 2005, Sapore had earned its first Michelin Bib Gourmand, cementing its place in Italy's culinary scene.

Today, Sapore has locations across the globe, but our commitment remains the same: serve authentic Italian cuisine made with love, using the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques passed down through generations.
